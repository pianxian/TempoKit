Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start a session?

Open TempoKit, choose a session type, confirm your target or Smart Match mode, and tap Start.

Why is cadence not being tracked?

Make sure motion permissions are enabled and that your device or supported AirPods motion source is available.

How do I manage subscriptions?

You can restore purchases or manage your subscription directly from the app and from your App Store account settings.
